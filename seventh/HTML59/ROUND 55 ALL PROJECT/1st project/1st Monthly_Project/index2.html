About IsDB-BISEW

IsDB-BISEW has been operating a number of educational programmes for the underprivileged Muslim youth since 2003. Meanwhile, the programmes have earned enormous reputation and success in local and overseas job markets.

All the programmes have been maintaining an average of 92% direct job placement of beneficiaries. It is really appreciable and praiseworthy contribution towards the economy of Bangladesh and strongly recommended for continuing the programmes in broader and extended domain.
